Onboarding the Masses
to the Blockchain Ecosystem

Onbloc is a blockchain software development firm
based in Seoul, building Gnoswap and Adena Wallet

Vision-Driven

Onbloc is a R&D company driven by a vision of removing the boundaries
that exist in onboarding the masses to the Web3 ecosystem. We focus on
building the core infrastructures and tools such as DeFi applications,
nodes, and wallets to accelerate the mass adoption of blockchain.

Gnoland-Dedicated

Gnoland is a public blockchain initiative led by Jae Kwon, the creator of
Cosmos Network and Tendermint. As an active contributor to the Gnoland
ecosystem, Onbloc is committed to developing Gnoland-native dapps, operating
a Gnoland validator, and investing in aspiring projects building on Gnoland.
